Club Med

Bringing a world-class mountain resort
to life through photography and film.

Sector

Luxury Hospitality

Services

Photography, Videography

A resort that
earns its
altitude.

Nestled in the mountains of Charlevoix, Club Med's resort is one of North America's premier ski and wellness destinations. Citizen North was commissioned to capture the full breadth of the property — from the powder-white slopes to the intimate warmth of its interiors — in a way that would speak to both adventure seekers and luxury travellers.

The Challenge

Four seasons.
One story.

Club Med Charlevoix is not just a ski resort — it transforms with the seasons. Our brief was to produce content that would work year-round across advertising, social, and editorial channels without feeling like a collection of unrelated shoots.

The challenge was capturing the visual continuity of the property across drastically different conditions: the drama of deep winter snowfall, the warmth of spring thaws, the lush greens of summer trails, and the blazing foliage of autumn.

Our Approach

Light, mood,
and patience.

We returned to Charlevoix across three separate visits spanning ten months. Rather than working against the seasons, we built each shoot around the specific light and mood each time of year naturally provides — blue-hour winter skies, golden-hour summer evenings, and the saturated warmth of October colour.

For the video component, we structured a narrative arc that follows a guest from arrival through to departure — letting the property reveal itself organically rather than through a series of disconnected highlights.

The Work

Details that
make guests stay.

The final deliverables included over 400 photography assets across interior, exterior, lifestyle, and food & beverage categories, along with a three-minute brand film and a series of shorter clips optimised for digital advertising.

All assets were delivered in multiple formats and aspect ratios — ready for use across Club Med's global marketing channels, from full-page magazine spreads to Instagram Stories.

"Citizen North captured not just our resort — they captured the feeling of being here."
— Marketing Director, Club Med Charlevoix
